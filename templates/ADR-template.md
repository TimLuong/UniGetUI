## Template Usage Instructions

When using this template:

1. **Copy this template** to your ADR directory as `ADR-XXXX-title.md`
2. **Use sequential numbering** (e.g., ADR-0001, ADR-0002, etc.)
3. **Fill in all sections** - remove sections that aren't applicable
4. **Be specific and detailed** - future you will thank present you
5. **Update the status** as the decision progresses
6. **Link to related ADRs** to maintain context
7. **Archive, don't delete** - even superseded ADRs provide valuable history
8. **Review regularly** - set up periodic reviews to validate decisions

### Status Definitions

- **Proposed**: Decision is being considered but not yet made
- **Accepted**: Decision has been approved and is active
- **Deprecated**: Decision is no longer recommended but may still be in use
- **Superseded**: Decision has been replaced by a newer ADR (link to it)
